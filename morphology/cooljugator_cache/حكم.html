Conjugation of حكم

This verb can also mean the following: establish solidly, put, be, command, bridle, hit upon, appoint, treat, deliver a sentence, deliver, restrain, obtain, bid, appoint as a judge, bid to give full judgment, be a physician, be learned, hit, wise, protect, give, put the bit to a bridle, happen, treat medically, judge between, establish, give full powers, keep back from evil
