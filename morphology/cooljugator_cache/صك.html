Examples of صك

... أحتاج إلى صك الأوعية الدموية

I need vascular instruments...

I think "gouged" is a good description using some kind of curved instrument of Probably the hoof knife.

أنا لستُ صك الزوال

I am not the instrument of their demise.

أو أي صك؟

Or any instrument?

Vile instrument!

It's because I'm resting my instrument.
