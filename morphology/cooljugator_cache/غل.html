Conjugation of غل

This verb can also mean the following: suffer, enter, join together, be put into, yield crops, fetter, embezzle,thirst for revenge, shackle, be, bind, chain, suffer from violent thirst, be admitted, yield, put, turn from what is right, put on the undergarment illa, thirst, turn
